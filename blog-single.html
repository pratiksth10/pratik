Grow that Mo!

Posted on: June 21, 2013Posted under: Announcements

The Movember* Competition is a chance for you to let your hair down and really get growing! Throughout the month of November we encourage you to grow your facial hair into a moustache and upload your progress to this thread. It doesn’t matter if you are a guy or a girl, if you struggle in the facial hair department you will have to get creative!

How it works

It all starts on the 1st of November. If you would like to participate you must upload a BEFORE and AFTER photo of your Mo.

  • The BEFORE photo should be a fresh faced picture, with not a hair follicle in sight!
  • The AFTER photo should be uploaded by the 1st of December (CST) and display your glorious Mo effort from the month!

What’s up for grabs?

This year YOU will chose the winning Mo. Staff will select the top 3 from the thread and then the community will vote on who they think should win the Golden Mo award. The winner will get a Gold Mo badge and $50 in Marketplace credit. Two runners-up will win $25 Marketplace credit each! That’s quite a prize. Every person who Grows a Mo and posts a picture in this thread will be awarded a black Mo badge.
